Hello Potential Sponsors!

This year, Bitcamp will be hosted virtually from April 9th to 11th, 2021! This enables Bitcamp to go global and bring even more creative and driven minds together. Student developers, designers, and thinkers of varying technical ability and experience will congegrate online and spend 36 hours cultivating websites, apps, and hardware projects that strive to better their communities. Hackers and their teaqms also develop a multi-disciplinary mindset. Not just focusing on the coding aspects of their projects, they are challenged to learn new skills including projects and user-friendly design. Bitcamp presents an excellent opportunity for recruitment and tech mentorship.

We’d love for you to review the various tiers of sponsorship below. Additionally, Bitcamp is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, meaning that any and all contributions are fully tax-deductible.

Please let us know if you are interested in being part of Bitcamp or have any questions regarding sponsorship. You can reach us at sponsorship@bit.camp. We hope to see you at the campfire!
